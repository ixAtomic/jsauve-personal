Challenge Attended 2022

The first time I attended this challenge was in 2022.

During the first event me and my group learned a lot and were able to get quite a bit done in 24 hours

We kind of split our team in half and part of us worked on an android app and the other worked on the desktop app

The goal was to track the space station across the sky in realtime. We were able to, using javascript three.js library, display earth and get a linear orbit display around the planet

We were also able to get a pretty sophistaced web UI working as well

The android app was able to display the space station in a rather klunk orbit
